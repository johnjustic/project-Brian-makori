What We Do

At Camara, we believe that access to technology and digital literacy is essential for empowering communities and creating positive change. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing refurbished computers and comprehensive digital literacy training to schools and educational institutions across Ethiopia, Kenya, Zambia, Lesotho, Tanzania, and Ireland.

Join Our Cause

By navigating our website, you'll discover the inspiring stories of how Camara is making a difference in communities around the world. We invite you to explore the various activities we're involved in, from community outreach to computer refurbishment services. Together, we can ensure that no one is left behind in the digital age.
