About Camara

Camara is a social enterprise dedicated to empowering communities through technology and education...

Since our establishment, we have refurbished and distributed thousands of computers to schools and institutions across Africa and Ireland...

Our commitment to sustainability, education, and impact has fueled our growth and garnered support from donors and partners around the world...

Our Vision

At Camara, we envision a world where every individual, regardless of their background or location, has equal access to technology...

We strive to create a positive impact by fostering digital literacy, enabling lifelong learning, and empowering communities...
