About Camara

Camara is a social enterprise dedicated to empowering communities through technology and education. Our journey began with a vision to bridge the digital divide and provide equal opportunities for learning and growth.

Since our establishment, we have refurbished and distributed thousands of computers to schools and institutions across Africa and Ireland. Through our innovative digital literacy programs, we equip individuals with the skills they need to thrive in the digital age.

Our commitment to sustainability, education, and impact has fueled our growth and garnered support from donors and partners around the world. Join us in our mission to create a brighter future for all through technology.
